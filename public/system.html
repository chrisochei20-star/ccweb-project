CCWEB 3-Part System Prototype

1) Google Maps AI Search -> 2) AI Analyzer -> 3) Contact + Proposal + Payment

Platform fee is fixed at 8% of each deal; applicant receives the remainder after client confirms completion.

1) Google Maps AI Search

Find businesses in any city worldwide (prototype dataset + AI query scoring).

    2) AI Analyzer (Claude-style)

    Reads reviews + online presence and returns a 6-card problem report.

    Applicant Profile + AI Income Engine Fit

    Secure profile setup for skills, capacity, certificates, and AI business compatibility scoring.

          3) Contact + Proposal + Payment

          Submit proposal, client pays in CCWEB escrow, then payout auto-releases when deal is marked done.

          How the payout logic works

          • Client payment goes to CCWEB escrow.
          • CCWEB fee = 8% of total deal amount.
          • Applicant payout = 92% after done confirmation.
          • Status transitions: proposal_submitted -> in_escrow -> released.
          • Confirmation is role-gated and token-protected: role=client + X-CCWEB-PAYROLL-TOKEN.

          This prototype uses in-memory storage for demo behavior.
